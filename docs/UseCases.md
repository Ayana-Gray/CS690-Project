## Business Requirement
This software will empower Sofia to tackle daunting goals of language learning, half-marathon training, and novel writing by breaking them into manageable daily actions. It ensures she stays motivated by tracking her daily progress across all goals and turning her overwhelming ambition into consistent achievement.
